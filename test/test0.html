Provably Fair

Provably Fair is an algorithm implemented by Skin.Club to demonstrate that all rolls and outcomes in case openings and other mechanics are genuinely random and remain unaltered. This assures users that there is no manipulation of the system, and every participant has an equal chance of obtaining an expensive skin from a CS2 case. With PF, predicting future outcomes is impossible; however, users can verify previous results.

This approach ensures the fairness and mathematical accuracy of all results, whether they are wins or losses, and eliminates the possibility of individuals manipulating expected numbers. Skin.Club is proud to offer one of the most transparent and honest PF systems in the market.

  • At Skin.Club, you have the ability to review the complete history of all rolls and seed changes. This feature ensures that the customization of hit probabilities for specific users is impossible.
  • Skin.Club allows users to inspect the hits and game results of other players, confirming the authenticity of their outcomes. This transparency guarantees that other players' game results cannot be falsified.

Furthermore, Skin.Club is unique in offering the ability to check the history of both client and server seeds. This data is openly available for verification by anyone at any time.
